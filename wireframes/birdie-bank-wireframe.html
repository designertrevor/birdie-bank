How to play Banker
3–8 players · Rotating banker each hole
Overview
One player is the Banker each hole. All other players bet against the banker. The player with the lowest net score wins their individual matchup against the banker.
Each hole
  1. Select the banker for the hole
  2. Each non-banker sets their bet amount
  3. Any player can double their bet (2×)
  4. If a player doubles, the banker may double back (4×)
  5. Enter gross scores for all players
  6. Net scores are compared vs the banker
Scoring
  • If a player's net score beats the banker → player wins their bet
  • If the banker's net score beats a player → banker wins that bet
  • Tied → push, no money changes hands
  • The banker's total is the sum of all individual matchups
Rotation modes
  • Fixed — same banker all round
  • Every hole — rotates through players
  • Every 9 — one banker per nine
  • Player choice — group picks each hole
Strategy
Being banker is high risk, high reward — you play against everyone at once. When you're hot, being banker is very profitable. When you're cold, losses stack fast.
